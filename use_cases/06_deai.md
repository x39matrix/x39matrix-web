## THE CURRENT STATE OF AI

Artificial Intelligence is the most centralized technology ever created. Three companies control the frontier models: OpenAI (GPT), Google DeepMind (Gemini), and Anthropic (Claude). Users cannot verify how decisions are made. Training data is opaque. Inference is a black box. AI hallucinations, bias, and errors cannot be audited by external parties.

The AI industry generates $200+ billion annually but operates on trust: "Trust that our model is not biased. Trust that your data is not being used. Trust that our safety measures work." There is no cryptographic proof. There is no independent verification.

On-chain AI attempts:
- Bittensor (TAO): Decentralized AI marketplace where miners compete to provide inference. Good tokenomics but individual inference results are not cryptographically verified.
- Ritual Network: Coprocessor for verifiable AI inference. Promising but high latency and cost.
- Giza: Zero-knowledge proofs for AI model verification. Mathematically rigorous but extremely expensive to compute.
- Modulus Labs: zkML (zero-knowledge machine learning). Early stage, limited model complexity.
- ICP DeAI: DFINITY's initiative for on-chain AI inference. Growing rapidly with the Caffeine platform.

The fundamental gap: no existing solution provides real-time, on-chain AI that is autonomous, verifiable, and integrated into a full protocol architecture.
